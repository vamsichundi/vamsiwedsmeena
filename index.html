Explore the Area

Since the celebration falls on a weekend, here are a few of our favorite scenic spots and beaches near The Woodlands & Houston. Tap any card for directions.

Scenic Spots & Urbanscapes

Sugar Land · 4.8★

Hanuman Statue (Statue of Union)

A majestic 90-foot Lord Hanuman statue at the Sri Ashtalakshmi Temple — one of the tallest in the U.S. and a deeply uplifting sight.
Houston · 4.7★

Gerald D. Hines Waterwall Park

A stunning 64-foot cascading water wall framed by oak trees near the Galleria — a favorite Houston landmark for photos.
Houston · 4.6★

Space Center Houston (NASA)

The official visitor center of NASA's Johnson Space Center — real rockets, moon rocks, and interactive exhibits for all ages.
Downtown Houston · 4.7★

Downtown & Discovery Green

A lively 12-acre park in the heart of downtown with lawns, water features, and events — a great base to explore the city.

Beaches by the Coast

Galveston · 4.6★

Galveston Island State Park

Wild Gulf-side shoreline and calm bay waters, with trails, birding, and the island's famously soft golden sunsets.
Galveston · 4.4★

Stewart Beach

A family-friendly beach with easy parking, restrooms, chair rentals, and food trucks — a classic Galveston day out.
Seabrook · 4.4★

El Jardin Beach

A quiet, scenic bay beach with free parking and lots of space to spread out — calm, clean, and wonderfully low-key.
Galveston · 4.5★

Moody Gardens

A rainforest pyramid, aquarium, and seasonal attractions all in one spot — a fun outing for families and kids.

Galveston's beaches are roughly a 1.5–2 hour drive south of The Woodlands — a perfect day trip to round out the weekend.
