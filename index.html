Our Story

From two families to one

We are so happy to share the joy of our wedding with you. With the blessings of our parents and elders, and in the presence of Lord Venkateswara at the Hindu Temple of Woodlands, we begin this new chapter together.

Our wedding will follow traditional Telugu Hindu customs — from the Mehendi and Haldi ceremonies to the sacred wedding rituals at the Muhurtham. We would be honored to have you celebrate with us and bless our union.
